# Usage (preview)

You can click around the static preview now to understand the flow.

## Try the preview
1. Open `web/index.html` in your browser.
2. Write a sample post in the composer. Toggle the “Treat pasted X link as a quote” switch to see where the quote option will live.
3. Pick sample platforms and groups (chips). These mirror your requested accounts (X main + alt, Instagram, YouTube, Shorts, TikTok, Bluesky, Threads).
4. Look at the weekly calendar cards to see how scheduled posts will appear; drag-and-drop will be added next.
5. Check the validation rail to see upcoming guardrails (media size checks, per-platform limits, quote detection, OAuth requirements).

## Upcoming interactive features
- Drag-and-drop scheduling with collision checks.
- Per-platform field validation (caption lengths, aspect ratios, Shorts vs. YouTube duration rules).
- Move/clone actions that keep media cached so you don’t re-upload.
- AI helpers for captions, hashtags, and timing suggestions.
